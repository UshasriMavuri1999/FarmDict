  • What is FarmDict for?

    FarmDict is farmer's friend. Its a platform that brings all the farmers under one roof. The app allows you to know the foroecast of weather for next 7 days and hourly forecast of temperature for next 24 hours based on your current location , you get to know about current daily price of various commodities from various markets in the country, discussion form to discuss with other farmers for better productivity, can find the details about your nearest soil testing labs, can know about pests, diseases and cure. This app answers the farmer basic questions.

  • Will i have to pay?

    FarmDict provides its services with no cost included.
